How to play?

Click on a cell to reveal it.

You think you found a mine? right click to flag it.

If you are right, you get a point.

You have 3 lives.

Cheats

Click on the magnifying glass to reveal a safe cell.

Click on the light bulb to reveal a hint.

Click on the mega hint to reveal a MEGA hint.

Exterminator removes 3 mines.
